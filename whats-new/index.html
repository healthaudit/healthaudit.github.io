Update

In 2016 we have
  • adopted the name Health Audit Group for our umbrella organisation
  • appointed a Director of Operations and Compliance
  • continued the process of accreditation to Human Services Quality Framework (HSQF) – Queensland
  • begun implementation of the transition to IEC/ISO 17021:2015
  • implemented a new version of the DAA Handbook
  • undergone a surveillance audit by our accrediting body in October 2016
  • expanded our contract audit pool
  • increased our client base
